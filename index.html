Meet the team

Milano Coffee is located in the tiny village, Felsőzsebalja. We just call it Milano to make it sound more classy.

Located conveniently just down the train-station, Milano Coffee stands unique in our village. Being the only coffe in Felsőzsebalja, we don't have to be affraid of competition (too much honesty might just end our business though).
